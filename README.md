# Olist E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end e-commerce data analysis of the Olist dataset using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to analyze sales performance, customer distribution, product performance, payment behavior, and key business trends through an interactive dashboard.

## Tools & Technologies

- SQL
- Python
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Analysis
- Data Visualization

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level view of overall business performance, including:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Review Score
- Average Delivery Days
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Orders by Payment Type
- Orders by Order Status
- Review Score Distribution
- Yearly Order Trend

### 2. Customer & Geographic Analysis

Analyzes customer distribution and geographic performance, including:

- Customer Distribution by Location
- Top States by Customers
- Top Cities by Customers
- Top States by Orders
- State and Year Filters

### 3. Sales & Product Analysis

Provides detailed sales and product insights, including:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Products
- Average Order Value
- Top Product Categories by Revenue
- Revenue by Payment Type
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Top Products by Revenue
- Top Categories by Average Product Price
- Product Category and Order Year Filters

## Key Business Insights

- The business generated approximately 16.01M in total revenue.
- Around 99K orders were recorded from approximately 96K unique customers.
- Credit card is the dominant payment method.
- São Paulo represents the largest customer and order market.
- Revenue shows significant monthly fluctuations, with a notable decline around September.
- A small group of product categories contributes a significant share of overall revenue.
- Customer review scores are predominantly high, with score 5 being the most common.
